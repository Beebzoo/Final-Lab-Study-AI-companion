During a group project at university, one of my group members was not contributing as much as the rest of us. I felt frustrated because it seemed unfair that others had to take on extra work. therefore, I decided to approach the person and asked him what is going on. He told me that he is struggling with his part and did not really have the time to complete it yet. I suggested that we can meet as a group and all help him with his part but nevertheless it is important that he takes some time to efficiently work on the project. After this conversation, he was able to finish the part.
