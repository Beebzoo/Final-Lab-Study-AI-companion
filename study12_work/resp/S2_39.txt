A couple of weeks ago I had a disagreement with one of my groupmembers. We were working on a shared presentation, and I felt like me and 2 others of the group were carrying most of the workload. At first, I did my job and kept quiet, but later I started to feel really frustrated. I needed to speak with him so I did. I told him how unfair it was that we were doing all the work for him. At first he reacted a bit defensive, but in the end he said that he had not realized how much we were doing for him, and he agreed to do more work. It felt uncomfortable at first to chat to him and tell him how we as a group felt, but in the end it was the best solution.
