I had a difficult interaction where I was trying to give a friend advice on how to handle the stresses of their new job. I was offering a lot of practical suggestions and trying to reassure them that everything was going to be okay and this was simply a difficult adjustment period. They got angry at my advice l saying I wasn’t trying to help comfort them or validate their feelings. My suggestions made them feel stupid and my reassurance made it seem like I wasn’t understanding their emotions. I first got defensive because I thought it was rude to respond to someone trying to help you in that way, but upon reflection I think they just had specific needs and were trying their best to communicate them. When I processed this I was able to calm down and be there more meaningfully for them during this difficult transition.
