Recently i had a disagreement with a colleague during a group task because we had different ideas about how to approach the work. At first, the conversation got a bit tense because we both felt strongly about our own suggestions. I decided to pause and listen more carefully to their point of view instead of reacting. 
After that, i explained my perspective more calmly and focused on finding common ground. In the end, we combined parts of both ideas, and the final result was actually better than what either of us had planned individually.
