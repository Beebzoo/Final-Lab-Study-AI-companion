A challenging situation I faced was during a group project at school. One of our teammates stopped participating and wasn’t responding to messages, and the deadline was getting close. At first, it was frustrating because it affected the whole group’s progress.
I decided to take the initiative and reorganize our tasks so that the remaining members could divide the missing work more efficiently. I also reached out personally to the student who had stopped responding, and it turned out they were struggling with personal issues. We agreed on smaller, manageable tasks for them to complete.
