2 weeks ago, I had to be a discussion leader for my MOM course, with another guy from my class. Since I am not comfortable doing the Pre-Discussion of the task, I split the work and assigned myself the Post-Discussion, which was much longer than the Pre-Discussion. I asked my co-discussion leader if he was OK with it, and he told me he was. However, when the tutorial arrived, we both saw that my part was actually so much bigger than his, that I had to speak for 1 hour and 45 minutes, and him only 15 minutes. At the end of the tutorial, our tutor asked us to stay a bit longer so she could talk with us. I was really scared. She asked if everything was OK in the group, because I had to work way more than him, and she thought he was the one that didn't want to work and let me do it by myself. I felt really bad for him, because he didn't do anything wrong, he just followed my instructions. So I quickly apologized and explained the situation to the tutor. Fortunately, she understood, so we were able to leave without any problems.
