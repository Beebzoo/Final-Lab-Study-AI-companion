I recently had a pretty tense disagreement with a close friend regarding some shared travel plans that fell through at the last minute. I felt frustrated because I’d already put in a lot of effort, but instead of snapping, I took a day to cool off before explaining my perspective calmly. We eventually sat down and cleared the air, which actually made our friendship feel more solid in the long run. It was a good reminder that taking a beat before responding can save a lot of unnecessary drama.
