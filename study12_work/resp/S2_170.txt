Yeah, I actually had a tough situation during a group project at university. One of my classmates and I couldn’t agree on how to start: I wanted to outline first, and she just wanted to start writing ‘’in medias res’’. At first, I got annoyed, but then I realized she was just stressed about deadlines and wanted to start right away.
So instead of arguing, I asked her why she felt that way and really listened. We ended up compromising: made a quick outline together, then started writing. It worked out great. Honestly, it reminded me how much just listening and staying calm can turn a disagreement into teamwork.
