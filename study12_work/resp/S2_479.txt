Honestly, a few weeks ago I had an issue with a guy in my group project.
He kept turning in his part late and I always ended up staying up late to piece everything together… I was pretty stressed and annoyed.

So I messaged him to talk about it. I was a bit nervous it might get awkward, but he explained he was also working a lot of hours at his job and was struggling to keep up.

We decided to break the work into smaller pieces and set up two quick check-ins every week. It actually helped — he became more consistent and I was way less stressed.

I learned it’s better to bring things up early and stay calm instead of letting it build up until it blows up.
