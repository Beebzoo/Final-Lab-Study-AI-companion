During a group project at university, one of your teammates wasn’t contributing equally and kept missing deadlines. This created tension among the group because it affected everyone’s grade. You decided to talk to the teammate privately. You explained how their lack of participation was impacting the team and asked if something was preventing them from contributing. After the conversation, they admitted they were struggling to understand the assignment. You offered to help explain the parts they found difficult, and together, the group was able to finish the project successfully.
