I normally don't like to criticise, but i did say a few words on what i think he did wrong, and how exactly it though he could improve the presentation to make it more interesting, engaging, and to convey certain information in a better way. His body language made me feel like he didn't take the constructive criticism too well, but i know that even though he didn't like it, he will take these things into account in future presentations, and this will be a net positive.
