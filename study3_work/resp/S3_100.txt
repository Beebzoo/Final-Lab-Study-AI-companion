I guess one challenging situation I had recently was trying to figure out who to select as members of the executive board for a local community wellness-focused group I am currently co-president of. The other co-president and I had certain candidates in mind, but we were concerned about one of them taking on too much responsibility between an executive board position on our group and her other expected/likely commitments for next year. Ultimately, we decided to offer her the position contingent on her not taking any more long-term responsibilities that would involve extensive planning, and she liked being a part of our group so much that she happily accepted and turned down another position in another organization she was offered.
