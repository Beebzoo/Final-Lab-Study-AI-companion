I had a challenging interaction with a friend when I cancelled plans last minute. In the fall, I didn't value their time. I handled it by apologizing without making excuses, then explained what happened and asking how to make it up to her. I said, you're right to be upset. I messed up and I want to reschedule. We rescheduled, had a good time and cleared the air. I learned that knowing the impact first, then explaining works better than defending myself. It also reminds me to communicate earlier if plans might change.
