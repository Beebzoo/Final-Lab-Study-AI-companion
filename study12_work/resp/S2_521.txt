Recently, I have been having trouble telling my housemates to clean up after themselves in the kitchen. Everyone denies leaving a mess but the kitchen is always filthy. I finally saw someone cook and leave all their dirty pots and dishes in the sink without cleaning them. I handled this by knocking on their door and gently explaining that not cleaning up, makes it harder for everyone else to use the kitchen and the rest of us feel disrespected. He was understanding and apologized.
