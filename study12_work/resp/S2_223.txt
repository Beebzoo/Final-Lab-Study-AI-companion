My group and I have to make a presentation for our MOM class, and during our discussion of how we were going to go about making this presentation, we found ourselves disagreeing on which website to use. Some of the girls in the group insisted we used Canva whilst others suggested Google slides. To overcome this conflict of ideas, we thought it would be most fair to base it on majority vote. In the end we ended up going with google slides as most of the group members chose for it.
