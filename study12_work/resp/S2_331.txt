A recent challenging interaction I had was during a group project. One of my teammates wasn’t contributing much, and it was frustrating for everyone. Instead of getting angry, I talked to them privately and asked if something was wrong. They said they were struggling with time management, so we made a clearer plan and split the work better. After that, they started helping more, and we finished on time. It taught me that communicating calmly can really fix misunderstandings.
