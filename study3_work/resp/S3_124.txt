A recent challenging interaction I had was with someone who was quite frustrated and impatient during a conversation we were having. They just felt that their concerns weren't being taken seriously and it was starting to escalate a little bit. Instead of reacting straight away, I just focused on staying calm and made sure I was really listening to what they were saying and I acknowledged their frustration and made sure they felt heard, which really helped to de-escalate the situation and I just asked a few clarifying questions to make sure I fully understood the issue before I responded. Once I had a clearer picture of it, I explained things in a really simple and clear way and worked with them to find a solution that addressed their main concerns. By the end of the interaction, the tone had completely changed and the person was much more positive and for me that just reinforced how important it is to stay patient and focus on understanding what they're saying before you respond.
