Last week, I wanted to have some friends over for dinner in my garden. I live in a student house with 14 other students so out of consideration, I messaged our group chat to check if this would be ok with everyone. Shortly after I sent my message, another girl from our house messaged as well saying that she would also be hosting that same evening. She was persistent that we could both use the garden at the same time but I felt that it would be awkward with the two groups. I also felt that I had the right to use the garden since I had communicated first with everyone. I handled the situation by speaking to her in person later that day and suggesting that she could go with her friends in the kitchen and I could stay in the garden. I justified that my dinner was going be with a larger group and that for the next time we could both improve by communicating these things sooner to avoid potential conflict.
