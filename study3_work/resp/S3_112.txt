Recently, I had a hard time. I needed to have a serious conversation with my partner because I just didn't like his behaviors and some of the things he was doing. So, I had to carefully think about how I would approach the situation. And I didn't want to hurt his feelings, but I just wanted to ask him to kind of put me into consideration before he acted in certain ways.
