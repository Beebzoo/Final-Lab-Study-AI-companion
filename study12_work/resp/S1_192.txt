During the semester prior to this one, a supervisor at the internship I was working for got very upset with me for a simple mistake I made during my week. They were very condescending, but I did not want to sacrifice the job I had there. To remediate things,  I made sure to reply in a cool but apologetic tone that I was very sorry, I would fix the mistake, and that I wouldn't let it happen again. After this talk, it didn't happen again as I was much more vigilant.
