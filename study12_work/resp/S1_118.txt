Recently, I had to work on a group project for one of my classes. I always communicated clearly with my team but they did not do the same. I had to send multiple messages into our group chat for them to respond maybe once or twice. Usually, they would just be unresponsive. Finally, I told them I was going to do the whole project, submit it with just my name, and leave their names off of it. After saying this, they responded and we worked together to finish it. Although I do not like to speak with such a firm tone normally, I thought it was necessary in this situation because nothing else was working.
