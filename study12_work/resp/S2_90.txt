Recently, I had a challenging interaction with a teammate during a group project at University. We had different opinions about how to divide a task. I preffered a structured plan while they wanted a more flexible approach. At first I was frustraiting because I felt like my ideas weren't being heard. 
I decided to take a step back and ask them to explain their resoning more fully. Once I understood teir point of view I realized that combining both our ideas could actually work better. We agreed to sets a general plan but keep some flexibility for unexpected changes.
