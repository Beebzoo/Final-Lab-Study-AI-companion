A recent challenging interaction I had was during a group project at school. One of my teammates wasn’t contributing equally, which caused frustration among the rest of us. Instead of letting tension build, I decided to talk to them privately. I tried to stay calm and focused on the project rather than blame them. I asked if there was something preventing them from participating more and offered to redistribute tasks to make things fairer. After that conversation, they became more engaged, and we were able to finish the project smoothly. It taught me how much clear, respectful communication can solve problems
