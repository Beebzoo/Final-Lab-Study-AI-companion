Recently my partner and I had different expectations about weekend plan. I assumed we were spending time together. We had made other commitment. I realized that I was getting frustrated but I didn't want us to argue all weekend or let resentment build. I asked if we could talk once we about calm down. I used I statement. I felt disappointed because I was looking forward to our time. Instead of you never, I asked what was going on and we agreed to check with each other before making plans going forward. It cleared the air in 10 minutes instead of becoming a whole fight. Since then we had a quick Sunday check in about the weekend and it helped us to avoid the same issues. I learned that naming the feeling earlier is better than hollowing in it.
