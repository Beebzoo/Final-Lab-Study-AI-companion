After a house fire made my large household homeless and our real estate agent failed to rehome us as set as a condition in our rental agreement, we were instantly homeless. We were a large family consisting of my mother, my 3 brothers (1 of them has a disability), my two preschool-aged toddlers and my boyfriend. After staying a night at our relatives house, we sought the help from SASHS but unfortunately they said they could not find anywhere with the capacity to accommodate such a large amount of people. Everybody was scratching their head and disheartened. But then I thought out loud, what if they only had to look for a smaller group? My boyfriend and I are fine on our own, my brothers should stay with our relatives to be near their schools, so they only needed to find a place for my mum and two kids. The SASHS employees liked that idea and wondered why that didn't occur to them. They found alace instantly and so for a week or 2 my boyfriend and I lived in his ute. But at least my family was safe.
