I had a disagreement with my flatmate who was taking up too much space in the flat with his belongings, which were literally everywhere. I had no space for myself in the cupboards, fridge or other storage areas. To resolve this issue, I spoke to him as politely as possible, showing him what was bothering me so that I could put my things away. I was lucky that he understood my problem with his belongings, so we decided to divide all the furniture into two equal parts. Since then, everything has been much better.
