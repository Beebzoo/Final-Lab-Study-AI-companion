I had a disagreement with one of my roommates about cleaning shared spaces. It had been building up for a while—I noticed I was always the one taking out the trash and wiping down the counters, and even though it wasn’t a huge deal at first, it started to really bug me.

I didn’t want to come off passive-aggressive, so I tried to bring it up calmly one evening. I said something like, “Hey, can we talk about how we’re dividing up chores? I’ve noticed I’ve been doing a lot of the kitchen cleanup lately and it’s starting to feel a little one-sided.”

At first, they got kind of defensive—like, “Well I’ve been super busy, it’s not intentional”—which I get. But I kept the tone neutral and just explained how it was affecting me. Eventually we agreed to a simple chore schedule and put it on the fridge.

It wasn’t a fun conversation, but I’m glad I brought it up. The vibe in the apartment feels way better now, and I think they respected that I addressed it directly instead of letting it stew.
