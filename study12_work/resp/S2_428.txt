Today, I had to do my laundry and usually in the weekend the laundry room is really busy. So after studying the entire day I finally decided to book a laundry machine and headed to the room. When I got there I found out that someone else had used my booking to do his own laundry. I got furious and started shouting at the person calling out how unethical and rude what he had done was. He explained to me that he was in a huge rush and that he would miss his train if he was any more late, and he needed to do laundry because his suit was among his dirty clothes. After calming down, I took a deep breathe and tried to understand his situation. At the end he offered to book for me the next laundry machine ,which I agreed upon and with no hard feelings the issue was resolved. Despite being rude at first, at the end of the day everybody is going through life in a different way, facing different issues, and all we can do is try to be understanding and direct more energy on the positive aspects rather than waste energy on minor, not important issues such as this.
