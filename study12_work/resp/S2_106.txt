My most recent challenge was, an interaction I had during a group project at university. Everyone did not wanted to be the first one to start to give ideas or to contribute first. So it was really hard to get the project rolling. When I made the first move and just put in my first idea that came into my mind, we all became more comfortable and started to exchange ideas. In that way I learned that by approaching people in a group and by showing empathy, it can make a collaboration much smoother.
