I recently had a very challenging experience with this client of mine. So, we had a difference in opinion in how I was handling her matter and it got so heated that I was actually so close to dropping her. But then I thought back to my training, I thought back to how one behaves in a professional manner. So, I calmed down and I managed to talk to her and we fixed the problem. So, the way you handle a challenging interaction with someone is by calming down because nothing works. You can't help, you can't fix anything if you're both overreacting and your emotions are high. So, you have to calm down and look at the matter objectively.
