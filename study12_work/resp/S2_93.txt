last week in a group project a teammate ghosted us until two days before the deadline. I was honestly pissed. I’d did most of the work and didn’t want my grade thrown away. I pulled him aside and just said: ‘You missed meetings, didn’t reply, and that put the rest of us at risk. Are you in or not?’ He got defensive.  He did some work, but not enough. So I finished the critical parts myself and gave him smaller pieces. Result: we delivered, my grade stayed safe, and I learned to call out problems early and protect my time. Not pretty, but effective.
