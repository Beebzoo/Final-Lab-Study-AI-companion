A few months ago, I was working on a group project, and one of my teammates suddenly stopped responding just before our deadline. At first, it was stressful because their part was pretty important.

Instead of panicking, I talked with the rest of the team, and we decided to split the missing work between us. I also messaged that teammate to check in, and it turned out they were just really overwhelmed. We adjusted things a bit so they could still help with smaller tasks.

In the end, we managed to finish everything on time. It taught me a lot about staying calm, communicating clearly, and adapting quickly when things don’t go as planned.
