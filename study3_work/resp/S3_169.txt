I had a challenging situation with my partner. He wanted to go out for a meal and shopping and drinks with his mother on a Friday night. She had suggested it, his mother. He was happy with it, but I wasn't. I felt that Friday night is couple time, especially when he's busy with hobbies and events over the weekend and works in the middle of the week. I wanted time with my partner. I told him very clearly that I wouldn't accept it, that I would go back to live with my parents if that was the way I was going to have to live with my opinion not being respected or making me feel like I don't matter. He went quiet and thought about what I said. And the next day, after speaking via text message with his parent, his parent actually agreed that Friday night is couple night and that they would go on a Thursday night to see each other instead. So my opinion was listened to. It was difficult because I had to put my relationship on the line to make my point, but it was thought about and it was respected.
