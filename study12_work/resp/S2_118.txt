Recently, I had a difficult situation with a friend. We were working together on a project, but we didn’t agree on how to do some parts. At first, I felt a bit frustrated and didn’t know how to tell them without causing a conflict.

So, I took a deep breath and  explained my ideas and why I thought they might work. Then I listened carefully to their perspective. We discussed our ideas and finally found a compromise that worked for both of us.

After that, the project went much smoother, and I realized that taking the time to talk calmly and really listen can really help in difficult situations
