Recently, I had a door-to-door salesman who knocked on the door rather than using the ring camera. I answered the door and he had a brochure in his hand and immediately was very pushy wanting to sell it. I said to him, I'm not interested, to which he replied, what aren't you interested in? So, I just repeated, I'm not interested and closed the door. He then hammered on the door, which was quite scary really being a woman on my own. I went to the window and gestured for him to leave the property, to which he did. And then that night, I had some damage done to the rear of my property and although it might have been just a coincidence, it really did quite shake me up. So, I did a little bit of research and found out which company he worked for and considered ringing and making a complaint. But then I thought, actually, this could just aggravate matters even further. So, I just took extra security precautions in the home and decided to put it behind me.
