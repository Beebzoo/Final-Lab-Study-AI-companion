A few months ago, I had a very difficult interaction with someone. I had to tell my stepfather that my mom had passed away. During that conversation, I had to speak to him knowing that his life would never be the same, and he was very shocked. I spoke to him gently, explained the situation itself, and then I talked about the future, about how we were going to overcome this hardship. I also mentioned the administrative matters — it wasn’t the perfect moment, but it helped him realize quickly what was happening. I had to put my own emotions aside in order to support him as best as I could.
