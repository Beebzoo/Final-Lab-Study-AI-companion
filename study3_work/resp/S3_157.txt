I got a phone call from his parents that he was in a coma after a massive brain bleed. I had to break this news to my daughters and get them to the hospital in time to say goodbye. It was horrible. I've never had to do anything that hard in my life, telling them something that terrible. My youngest daughter doesn't live here. She lives at the other end of the city with her partner. I had to contact her partner first and tell her to make sure she was with my daughter. She wasn't. She couldn't get home for about an hour and a half. I had to wait an hour and a half to break the news to my youngest, which felt horrible. She didn't know. It also felt horrible telling her and console my oldest at the same time. I don't think that I have even processed it yet. I'm still in shock.
